# Significant Figures

Publication-quality figures from earthworm pilot 2018 microbiome analysis.

## Figure 1: Beta Diversity - Gut Compartment
NMDS ordination of gut microbiome (n=16) showing Bray-Curtis distances. Control (light green) vs Roundup (dark green). Stress=0.445, R²=0.0044, p=1.000 (NS).

## Figure 2: Beta Diversity - Soil Compartment
NMDS ordination of soil microbiome (n=6) showing Bray-Curtis distances. Control (light brown) vs Roundup (dark brown). Stress=0.373, R²=0.0309, p=1.000 (NS). Underpowered but consistent with Gut non-effect.

## Figure 3: EPSPS Class Distribution by Treatment
Class I (Sensitive) and Class II (Resistant) relative abundance by treatment and compartment (2×2 grid). Mann-Whitney U tests. No significant treatment effect in either compartment.

## Figure 4: Alpha Diversity by Compartment and Treatment
Shannon diversity and observed richness stratified by compartment. Compartment effect significant (p<0.05). No treatment effect (p>0.05).
